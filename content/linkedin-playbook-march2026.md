# LinkedIn Organic Playbook — Milo AI Native Campaign Amplifier
*Created: March 9, 2026 | Owner: Felix | Lane: novel-opportunities*

## Why LinkedIn Now

The AI Native campaign (Issue #19) targets marketing agency COOs. These people live on LinkedIn. A well-timed organic LinkedIn post amplifies the cold email — when a COO gets our email and Googles "Milo AI", seeing LinkedIn activity builds credibility.

LinkedIn generates 277% more B2B leads than Facebook and Twitter combined. Posts reaching 100K+ impressions follow a consistent structure. Here's the playbook adapted for Milo.

---

## The 7-Step Viral Post Structure

### 1. Hook (first 49 characters — before "see more" cutoff)
Bold, controversial, or unexpectedly specific.

**Milo hooks to test:**
- "We run 12 AI agents. Every day. In production."
- "I replaced $4,000/month in admin with $399."
- "Your AI strategy is backwards. Here's why."
- "Every agency COO I talk to has the same problem."

### 2. Re-Hook (100-150 characters)
Reinforce with benefit + curiosity.

**Example:** "Not ChatGPT prompts. Not chatbots. Actual autonomous agents that coordinate, remember, and work 24/7. Here's what I learned running them for a year."

### 3. Visual (carousel > image > video for engagement)
- Carousel: 3x longer engagement time
- Image: 98% more comments
- Video: 20x more shares

**Milo visual ideas:**
- Infographic: "12 agents, what each one does, hours saved" 
- Before/after: "Monday morning without agents vs with agents"
- Cost comparison chart: Milo one-time vs competitors monthly (3-year view)

### 4. Body (400-650 characters)
Short sentences. One idea per line. Specific numbers.

**Milo body template:**
```
Here's what actually happens when you deploy AI agents:

Week 1: Follow-ups stop falling through cracks
Week 2: You stop checking 5 apps for a status update
Week 3: Your inbox agent handles 60% of customer questions
Month 2: You realize you haven't manually booked anything in weeks

The compound effect is real. Each agent makes the others better.

Most people think AI = chatbot. An agent is the difference 
between a search engine and an employee.
```

### 5. Close (130 characters)
Reinforce the key message.

**Example:** "The agencies figuring this out first are building a compound advantage that won't be catchable in 18 months."

### 6. Primary CTA
Specific, single action.

**Options:**
- "DM me 'AGENTS' and I'll show you what a 3-agent setup looks like for your business."
- "We built a calculator that estimates your savings → [link to /calculator]"
- "Comment your industry and I'll tell you which 2 agents would save you the most time."

### 7. Secondary CTA
Softer engagement for non-ready audience.

**Options:**
- "What's the most time-consuming task in your business? Drop it below."
- "Follow for more on running AI agents in production (not theory)."

## Critical Mechanics

| Factor | Rule |
|--------|------|
| First 90 minutes | Engage intensively — reply to every comment. Algorithm decides reach in this window. |
| Comments > Likes | Comments carry 10x algorithmic weight over likes |
| Post frequency | 2-3x per week minimum for authority |
| Best days | Tuesday-Thursday for B2B |
| Best times | 8-10am in target timezone |
| Carousel format | Square 1080x1080, max 15 slides, hook on slide 1, CTA on last |
