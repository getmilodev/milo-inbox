## Email Voice Guidelines

- **Tone:** Direct, warm, never salesy. Like a smart friend giving honest advice.
- **Length:** Short. Every sentence earns its place. Respect their time.
- **CTA:** Always "reply to this email" — not a link to a form. Low friction.
- **Proof:** Reference "12 agents in production" and "we use this ourselves" — our key differentiator.
- **Honesty:** Admit what agents CAN'T do. "Not for everyone" builds trust.
- **No attachments.** No HTML templates. Plain text feels personal.
- **EARLYMILO25:** Mention in first cold touch and post-demo. Not in every email.
