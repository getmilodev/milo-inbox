# Milo Wedge Rebuild Design

**Date:** 2026-03-09

## Goal

Reposition Milo's public site around a narrower buyer: agency owners and lean professional-service firms already using AI casually, but still operating core workflows manually.

## Constraints

- Preserve the current visual system and layout patterns.
- Keep nav, footer, mobile menu, and page structure intact.
- Do not reintroduce the deprecated receptionist / answering-service positioning.
- Use customer-language pain points from the intel docs rather than AI-generic claims.

## Message Architecture

### Homepage

Purpose: change the top-level story from broad AI systems to a sharper operational wedge.

Key message:
- Your team already uses AI in fragments.
- The problem is not awareness; it is dependable systems.
- Milo turns proposals, reporting, follow-up, and research into owned workflows.

### Services page

Purpose: explain what Milo actually builds for this buyer and why it is different from "just use ChatGPT" or hiring a generic automation freelancer.

Key message:
- Replace scattered AI experiments with owned operating systems.
- Start with one bounded workflow if needed.
- Keep the ownership and handoff story, but make it concrete.

### Build page

Purpose: keep the existing interaction pattern while updating the options and outputs to feel relevant to agencies and professional-service firms.

Key message:
- Build a workflow blueprint, not a vague AI team.
- Recommend systems around proposal turnaround, reporting, follow-up, research, and delivery coordination.

### Demo page

Purpose: make the conversion moment feel like a working session for high-fit operators, not a generic sales call.

Key message:
- We will map the workflow drag in your firm.
- We will show what gets automated first.
- You leave with a clear first-system recommendation.

## Pricing Interpretation

- `$399` should be framed as a first-step offer, not an all-purpose custom deployment.
- `$2,499` remains the stronger "real build" tier.
- Copy should reduce the risk that buyers assume unlimited bespoke work at the lowest tier.

## Success Measure

The rebuild is successful if the wedge-specific positioning produces:

- a materially higher reply rate from high-fit outbound, or
- at least 5 qualified intro calls from the first 100 high-fit agency / professional-service targets within 14 days.
